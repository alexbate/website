Michael Benjamin Gale

I am a PhD student in the Computer Laboratory at the University of Cambridge where I am working on Rich types for system programming languages under the supervision of Alan Mycroft. I am a member of Darwin College.

My general research interests focus around functional programming, compilers and the design of programming languages, including their type systems. I am also interested in program correctness and optimisation.

I was previously an undergraduate student in the School of Computer Science at the University of Nottingham. If you are currently a student there, you may be looking for my G52MAL revision guide or the Guru Programme.
