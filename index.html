I am a PhD student in the Computer Laboratory at the University of Cambridge where I am working on Programming with effect hierarchies under the supervision of Alan Mycroft. I am a member of Darwin College.

My general research interests focus around functional programming, compilers and the design of programming languages, including their type systems. I am also interested in program correctness and optimisation.
